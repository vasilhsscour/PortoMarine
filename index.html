About Us

who we are

We are a small family business. And our goal is to offer the best service to our customers in the best way we can.


Where are we

We are located in Palouki, Amaliados. Next to the port and the beach. We are a few kilometers outside of Amaliada.


what we offer

Our store offers traditional cuisine, grilled meats of the hour, fresh fish. We also have an organized beach in the summer where one can enjoy the bath for free
